The Killers

'The Killers' is a band from Las Vegas, Nevada. They formed in 2001 with Brandon Flowers, Dave Keuning, Mark Stoermer, and Ronnie Vannucci Jr.

Studio Albums

The Killers have released four studio albums since 2001. In order, they are Hot Fuss, Sam's Town, Day & Age, and Battle Born.

Side Work

Brandon Flowers has completed various side work outside of the band. Check my favorite song of his off of the album 'Flamingo'.
